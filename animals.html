Eastern Wolf

Eastern wolf, also called the Algonquin Wolf, is what it claims to be, a wolf of the east. It is chocolate-yellow in coat color and possesses several natural gray-brown tones inside. The wolf of the east has a checkered past that began in Ontario, Canada. Since their discovery in 1963, there are a great number of opinions as to what an Eastern Wolf is. Some argue that they are a mixture of coyotes and grey wolves while others argue that they are actual wolves.
